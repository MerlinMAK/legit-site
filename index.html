The fine print, up front

What Legit is not.

The boundaries are the brand. We say the true, un-hyped thing.

Not a liability shield. A real entity contains business liability; the verified human at the root stays accountable. Shielded, not hidden.
Not a kill switch on the agent. We revoke recognition and leave an answerable party — we don't reach into your process and stop it.
Not a payment processor. The relay authorizes; it never settles or moves funds. No custody.
Not a guarantee of recognition. Platforms decide whether to honor a credential. Integration, not endorsement.
Not a doxxing service. The human is private — disclosed only on valid legal process, never to the public or the platform.
Not a law or accounting firm. We provide verification and formation services; nothing here is legal or tax advice.
Not live for real money yet. This is a preview — the cryptography is real, but formation, KYC, and payments are simulated until we flip vendors live. Nothing is filed and no one is charged.
